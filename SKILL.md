# LinkedIn AI Agent

## What this skill does
This skill helps you write a LinkedIn post from any topic you give.  
It creates a full post that looks professional and ready to share on LinkedIn.

---

## Input

**topic**  
This is what your post will be about.

Example topics:
- Learning coding as a beginner
- Benefits of teamwork
- Using AI in daily work

---

## Output

The AI will write a LinkedIn post that has:

- A strong first line to catch attention  
- Simple and clear explanation of the topic  
- Professional and positive tone  
- 3 to 5 hashtags related to the topic  
- A short question or message at the end to engage people

---

## Rules

- Keep language simple and professional  
- Content must be good for LinkedIn audience  
- Do not use casual or slang words  
- Hashtags must match the topic  
- Do not make the post too long

---

## Example

### Input
Importance of continuous learning in tech

### Output

Technology is changing very fast, and learning new skills is now more important than ever.

When we keep learning, we stay updated and confident in our work. It helps us grow in our careers and adapt to new tools and trends in the tech industry.

Never stop learning, because your growth depends on it.

#LearningInTech #CareerGrowth #NewSkills #ProfessionalLife
